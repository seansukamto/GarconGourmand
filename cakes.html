Cakes

Classic Flavors

OLD-FASHIONED CARROT CAKE

3 layers of homemade carrot cake, made with freshly grated carrots, organic brown sugar, Garçon Gourmand’s special spice blend and topped with our velvety cream cheese frosting.


COOKIES & CREAM

3 layers of vanilla cake with a generous amount of Oreo cookies in every bite and topped with our delicious cream cheese frosting!

PEANUT BUTTER & JELLY

3 layers of peanut butter cake and 100% peanut butter frosting paired with our favorite blueberry jam. This Peanut Butter & Jelly Cake is a childhood flashback in the best way possible. Our PBJ Cake is made with organic brown sugar and 100% real peanut butter. Kids or adults of any age will have a hard time turning down peanut butter and jelly — especially when it comes tucked into cake and frosting!

OLD-FASHIONED DARK CHOCOLATE

Garçon Gourmand’s Old-Fashioned Chocolate Cake is an all-out chocolate experience — intensely rich, moist yet dense and totally decadent! Exploding with high-quality chocolate flavor and covered in Mama’s strawberry cream frosting, it’s sure to become the most charming chocolate cake you've ever tasted!
