Personalisation

A personalized card offers a more meaningful approach to connect with your loved ones or individuals that matter to you. We offer an addition of personalized card for every order of cake. Whatever the occasion, adding a personalized card will be a meaningful keepsake. Whether it is a sweet love note to your spouse, or simple birthday wishes or greetings to your best friend — we will do our best to make each card special and meaningful. All of our cards are hand calligraphed. Calligraphy is one of the most intimate and personal forms of expression. The beautiful strokes and curves in each letter makes our personalized cards unique and beautiful.
